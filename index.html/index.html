Art is an everchanging and influencing object that can be manipulated and used in many different ways ,it is a way for artists to express themselves and their emotions, it can be used to tell stories, evoke feelings, and inspire change. It has been a part of human culture for thousands of years, and continues to evolve and influence society today. Learn the stories behind some of the most influential artists, their work, and how they have impacted the world of art and culture.



"I dream my painting and I paint my dream." -Vincent van Gogh

"Art is the elimination of the unnecessary." -Pablo Picasso

"Beauty perishes in life, but is immortal in art." -Leonardo da Vinci

"Everyone discusses my art and pretends to understand, as if it were necessary to understand, when it is simply necessary to love." -Claude Monet
